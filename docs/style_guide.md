# Style Guide

VikVec is aimed at local-first creation of isometric industrial training assets. The preferred visual style is a 2.5D or isometric interpretation of industrial scenes that feels clean, instructional, and easy to animate later.

- Use flat vector shapes where possible.
- Keep colors muted and corporate-friendly.
- Add soft shadows rather than dramatic lighting.
- Favor simple e-learning presentation over cinematic detail.
- Separate assets clearly so they can be reused in animation and scene assembly.
